About Us

The success story of Chaichology

Our Company Chaichology is a rapidly growing Tea-Chain, founded in 2025 by two young and passionate entrepreneurs – Nitish Kumar and Logesh,Chaichology is a fast-growing tea chain that blends tradition with innovation. Rooted in Tamil Nadu, we are renowned for our high-quality offerings and exceptional service. Our mission is to make chai not just a beverage but an experience—served sustainably in eco-friendly rice grain cups. With a vision to create a global presence while championing environmental responsibility, we are dedicated to preserving Mother Earth, one cup at a time.
